|About Us

The Melanin Institute is a pioneering international professional body focused on advancing aesthetics specifically tailored for skin of color.

Our Mission

The Melanin Institute aims to create an inclusive professional community that provides continuous education, mentoring, networking opportunities, and industry advancement for aestheticians across Nigeria, with particular emphasis on accessibility and ongoing skill development.

Continuous Education

Expert-led curriculum focusing on the unique physiological needs and aesthetic treatments for skin of color.

Professional Mentoring

Bridging the gap between seasoned experts and emerging practitioners through global mentorship programs.

Strategic Networking

Connecting professionals across Nigeria, Canada, and beyond to foster collaborative growth and industry influence.

Industry Advancement

Driving research and standards in melanin-focused dermatology and aesthetic medicine.
